>Physical Characteristics:

Cats are small, furry mammals with sharp claws and teeth, adapted for hunting and climbing. They have excellent hearing, a keen sense of smell, and good night vision

>Behavior:

Cats are known for their independent nature, but also enjoy interacting with humans, communicating through vocalizations, body language, and pheromones. They are naturally clean animals, grooming themselves meticulously

>Solitary Hunters, Social Animals:

Despite their solitary hunting instincts, cats are social animals and can form strong bonds with their owners
